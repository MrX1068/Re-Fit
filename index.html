#1 Dance studio.

Aeromint Studio is a dance school established in 1999. We aim to provide all students with the understanding and an insight into both technique and choreography to prepare them for the professional sector.

Ballet

Our ballet classes are suitable for all levels of experience and are an inspiring way to develop strength, flexibility and coordination. At our Dance School, we have designed ballet lessons for four different levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, and Professional.

Jazz-funk

Jazz-Funk is a hot commercial style of dance peppered with hip-hop, dancehall and lots of sass. Students should come with an open mind and ready to be challenged while having a good time. The class will start with a warm-up that includes cardio, as well as basic grooves.

  • Monday – Tuesday: 12.00 AM – 5.00 PM
  • Wednesday – Thursday: 10.00 AM – 3.00 PM
  • Friday – Saturday: 11.00 AM – 4.00 PM

Breakdance

Open to all experience levels, our breaking class teaches the steps to achieve many core breaking moves, covering foundations such as toprocking, power moves, and freezes. Through breaking down the movement and running drills, students will then practice applying these skills into their freestyle movement.

  • Monday – Tuesday: 9.30 AM - 11.45 AM
  • Wednesday – Thursday: 12.15 PM - 1.45 PM
  • Friday – Saturday: 2.30 PM - 4.15 PM
